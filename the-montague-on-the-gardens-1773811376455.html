About

The Montague on the Gardens

Nestled in the heart of London, The Montague on the Gardens is a proud part of The Red Carnation Hotel Collection, perfectly positioned near Covent Garden, the West End's premier theatres, and world-class shopping. Just steps from the British Museum, renowned for al fresco dining experiences and wonderfully fun pop-ups. Live jazz at Leopard Bar and traditional Afternoon Tea in the conservatory overlooking peaceful gardens.

Luxury 4-star hotel in Bloomsbury featuring seven private Georgian Grade II listed townhouses from 1806, offering classic British hospitality with elegantly designed guest rooms, signature British cuisine, and curated local experiences.
