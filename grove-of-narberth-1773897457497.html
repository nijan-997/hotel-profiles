About

Grove of Narberth

Grove of Narberth is an independent country house hotel with a history dating back to the 15th century. Hidden in the rolling Narberth hills, surrounded by meadows, woodland and mature gardens. The hotel features individually designed bedrooms with a modern twist on traditional Welsh country house style, a fine-dining 4 AA Rosette Fernery restaurant, and a more relaxed Artisan Brasserie. Known for exceptional hospitality, beautiful gardens spanning 26 acres, and sustainability initiatives.

Unbuttoned luxury, independent country house hotel offering sincere hospitality, best local ingredients, and escape in the heart of the Pembrokeshire countryside
