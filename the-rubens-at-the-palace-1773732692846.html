About

The Rubens at the Palace

A warm welcome to The Rubens at the Palace, part of The Red Carnation Hotel Collection. Located directly opposite the Royal Mews at Buckingham Palace, just a five-minute walk from Victoria Station. Steeped in history and stood on a site that was, in the 18th-century, part of the grounds of Buckingham Palace, The Rubens at the Palace stands as a living testament to its storied past.

A haven of luxury where history, comfort, and impeccable service converge for an unforgettable stay in the heart of London, celebrating 18th-century roots with warm and traditional British hospitality.
