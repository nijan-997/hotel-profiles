About

The Newt in Somerset

Immerse yourself in the timeless elegance of The Newt in Somerset, where Georgian heritage, honey-hued limestone buildings, and 300 years of curated orchards, lakes, woodlands, and scenic vistas create a tranquil countryside escape.

A working farm with rooms and 30-acres of formal gardens at its core, guests are invited to soak in the beauty of the Somerset landscape and make themselves at home.

Garden Tour

60-minute guided tour of the gardens led by a member of the team who tends them. Learn about the history and inspirations behind centuries of English garden design and how today's team are building on the bones of the past. Nurtured over the last 200 years by a succession of green-fingered experts.

Bee Safari

Take to the gardens with a member of the Bee Team to learn about bee colonies and pollinators on the estate: from their natural habitats to a bee's life cycle and why honeybees swarm. The safari concludes at the immersive Beezantium exhibit.

Garden Attractions

The Story of Gardening

Immersive exhibition exploring the history of gardening and the human impulse to shape beauty from the ground. Garden rooms and interactive exhibits transport you to gardens throughout time. Also serves light bites, sweet treats and estate-brewed coffee.

Open every day, 9:30am–4pm (Last entry 3:30pm)

Garden Attractions

Roman Villa Experience - Villa Ventorum

Expansive reimagining of a Romano-British villa, inspired by the unearthing of a settlement on the estate. Sits alongside an innovative and educational archaeological museum. Roman street food available 12pm–2:30pm. 20-minute walk from Threshing Barn. Buggies available on request with pre-booking.

Open every day, 9:30am–3:30pm

The Beezantium

Jewel box exhibition exploring intricate connections between bees, the land and humans. Nestled in the woodland surrounded by a specially planted apiary, houses a selection of wild bee colonies in observation hives.
