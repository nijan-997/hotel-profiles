About

The Montague on the Gardens

Nestled in the heart of London, The Montague on the Gardens is a proud part of The Red Carnation Hotel Collection, perfectly positioned near Covent Garden, the West End's premier theatres, and world-class shopping. Just steps from the British Museum, we are renowned for our al fresco dining experiences and wonderfully fun pop-ups. Enjoy live jazz at our chic Leopard Bar or savour a traditional Afternoon Tea in the conservatory overlooking peaceful gardens.

Seven private Georgian Grade II listed townhouses, originally constructed in 1806, form what is now the beautiful Montague on the Gardens hotel, located opposite the British Museum and surrounded by a tranquil private garden.
