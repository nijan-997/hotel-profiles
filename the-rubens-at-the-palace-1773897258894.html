About

The Rubens at the Palace

The Rubens at the Palace is a luxury hotel located directly opposite the Royal Mews at Buckingham Palace, part of The Red Carnation Hotel Collection. Steeped in history dating back to the 18th century when it was part of Buckingham Palace's grounds, the hotel offers individually designed rooms and suites, fine dining restaurants, and exclusive experiences. The hotel is family-run since 1997 and achieved Forbes Five-Star classification in 2018.

A warm welcome to The Rubens at the Palace, offering a blend of historic British hospitality with modern luxury, featuring warm smiles at every turn, sumptuous rooms and suites, and delicious cuisine overlooking the Royal Mews of Buckingham Palace.
