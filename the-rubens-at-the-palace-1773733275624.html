About

The Rubens at the Palace

The Rubens at the Palace is a luxury hotel located directly opposite the Royal Mews at Buckingham Palace, part of The Red Carnation Hotel Collection. Steeped in history on a site that was part of the 18th-century grounds of Buckingham Palace, it offers a blend of modern luxury and timeless charm with individually designed rooms and suites, exceptional dining experiences, and warm British hospitality.

A haven of luxury where history, comfort, and impeccable service converge in the heart of London, with meticulous attention to detail, personalized service, and celebration of the hotel's 18th-century roots.

What we offer

Afternoon Tea

Royal Afternoon Tea

From £70 per person

Savour our classic Royal Afternoon Tea: a delectable assortment of freshly baked scones, delicate finger sandwiches and delicate pastries. Our pastries include our signature crown, made with vanilla cheesecake, mango, passionfruit curd, and coconut sponge.

Royal Champagne Tea

From £90

Add a touch of sparkle to a timeless tradition with our Champagne Afternoon Tea in Westminster. Enjoy savoury sandwiches, freshly baked scones with clotted cream and preserves, handcrafted pastries, and a chilled glass of Champagne per guest, served in the elegant Palace Lounge overlooking the Royal Mews of Buckingham Palace. A premium non-alcoholic sparkling alternative is available.

Children's Afternoon Tea

£45 per child under 12

Our Prince and Princess Afternoon Tea includes bite-size sandwiches, freshly baked scones, pastries, and a choice of milkshake, hot chocolate, tea or soft drink. If you are staying with us, we also offer a children's Pastry Chef Experience for children under 12.

Vegan Afternoon Tea

At The Rubens at the Palace, vegan afternoon tea is treated with the same ceremony, creativity, and indulgence as its traditional counterpart—proving that plant-based dining can be every bit as refined. Carefully crafted by the hotel's pastry and culinary teams, the Vegan Afternoon Tea is a polished celebration of flavour, seasonality, and thoughtful sourcing, set just moments from Buckingham Palace.

Vegetarian Afternoon Tea

Our Vegetarian Afternoon Tea includes a tempting selection of vegetarian finger sandwiches, traditional scones served with homemade seasonal preserves and clotted cream, and a variety of vegetarian pastries and cakes.

Halal Afternoon Tea

Our Halal Afternoon Tea makes a few subtle ingredient substitutions, allowing you to enjoy the full Afternoon Tea experience. Our menu is inspired by our royal history and connections, including regal pastries such as 'The Crown'.

Gluten-Free Afternoon Tea

Our Gluten-Free Afternoon Tea is suitable for coeliacs, with each of our finger sandwiches served on gluten-free bread. Our Gluten-Free Afternoon Tea makes substitutions like for like, so guests won't miss out on any of our delicious taste combinations.
